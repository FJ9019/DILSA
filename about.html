About Us

Welcome to Diesel Injection Laboratory

Dieseil Injection Laboratory is a company that works in the Diesel Engine Industry since 1992.

Since its foundation this company has been dedicated to the sale and repair injection pumps, injectors, Turbochargers and their components and this, whether it is an automotive, naval, industrial application, or groups generators.

We strive to always be at the cutting edge of technological development in order to adequately meet the needs of our customers. Our control system of quality is more and more rigid. Diesel Injection Laboratory S.A has a constantly computerized system updated, an ever-growing parts inventory.

The continuing education of personnel and the acquisition of new production equipment and after-sales service sale are the main strengths of Diesel Injection Laboratory.
